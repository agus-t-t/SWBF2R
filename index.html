Remaster Project

A total conversion mod that revamps the maps, units, vehicles, weapons, effects renewing the looks of Battlefront II while keeping online compatibility. We even host our own dedicated server.

Realistic & Rezzed Maps

Rezzed Map: Literally a replacement of the original map, with HD textures, and in some cases added FX. The objective is to make a Rezzed version for every default map. All rezzed maps are online compatible. Remastered maps are the same but with improved models.

Realistic Map: Modifieded maps they have added props, models, effects, no consideration for filesize and my only interest will be making the environment as realistic as possible. This takes a lot of time so I won't make a Realistic version for every default map.

Improved Sides Mod

This mod will replace the stock sides (units) with upgraded player models, weapons and eventually vehicles. Sides classes, weaponry and other configurations (script related) will remain stock for preserving online compatibility.

You will find models from games like,Battlefront 3 (Free Radical),the force Unleashed and other Star Wars games plus custom ones.
